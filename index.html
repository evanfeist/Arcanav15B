Rules

🎯 Goal

Reach 60 points before your opponent.

🔄 Turn Flow

  • End of your turn: draw 1.
  • If you start a turn with ≤ 1 card: draw 3 (safety).
  • New game: deal 7 each. P1 starts.

🧩 Constructs (score to discard pile)

Pairs
  • Pair (2 of same rank): 2 pts
  • True Pair (same rank + same suit): 5 pts
Sets (3+ of the same rank)
  • 3 cards: 2 × rank + 2 pts
  • 4 cards: if all four suits present → 4 × rank, else 3 × rank
  • 5+ cards: 3 × rank + 2 pts
  • Suit bonus applies if all non-Page cards are the same suit (see Suit Bonuses). With Pages, suit bonus only applies if size ≥ 5.
Runs (3+ in sequence, same suit)
  • Score = sum of ranks + +1 per card
  • Pages (Jokers) can fill gaps; if any Page is used: no suit bonus and −1 point per Page
Using Pages (Jokers) in any non–Page-only construct
  • For each Page used: discard one extra other card (Page cost)
  • For each Page used: total points get −1 (shown in activity log & preview)
Page-only plays (discard these Pages to score)
  • 2 Pages → 4 pts
  • 3 Pages → 7 pts and draw 3
  • 4 Pages → 15 pts and refill hand to 7

♥ ♣ ♦ ♠ Suit Bonuses

  • ♥ Hearts: +3 pts
  • ♣ Clubs: take an extra turn (cap 2 in a row)
  • ♦ Diamonds: roll d6 → steal 1/2/3 points from opponent’s score
  • ♠ Spades: steal up to 2 random cards from opponent’s hand

Suit bonus applies to Constructs where all non-Page cards share that suit (see Pages notes under Sets/Runs).

🎭 Actions (6 / 7 / 9)

  • 6: Draw 2
  • 7: Steal 1 random card (Ace can attempt to block)
  • 9: Swap — give 1, take 1 (Ace can attempt to block)
    • If 9 is blocked: your “give” card is discarded
    • If 9 is not blocked: your “give” card goes to the defender

🂱 Aces (Defense & Counter)

  • Against 7/9: roll d6 → 1–3 fail (action proceeds), 4–6 block and you gain +1 point
  • Against a Sigil: discard 2 Aces to auto-block (Sigil is discarded)

👑 Sigils (J / Q / K)

  • Points: J +1, Q +2, K +3
  • Draw: J/Q +1, K +2
  • Opponent may auto-block by discarding 2 Aces

📦 Discard

Discard any 1 card to gain +1 point.

How to Play

1) Build Constructs for points

Make Pairs, Sets, or Runs. Pages (Jokers) can help, but may cost points and extra discards.

2) Actions (6/7/9)

6 draws 2. 7 steals 1 (Ace can try to block). 9 swaps a card (Ace can try to block).

3) Sigils (J/Q/K)

Choose Points (J+1/Q+2/K+3) or Draw (J/Q +1, K +2). Opponent can auto-block by discarding 2 Aces.

4) Suit bonuses

One-suit Constructs (no Pages) gain bonuses: ♥ +3 • ♣ extra turn • ♦ steal points • ♠ steal up to 2 cards.
